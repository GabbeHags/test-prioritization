Test Case Description for printing images

Purpose

Determine that Seamonkey prints various images properly

Initial Conditions

None

Steps/Description

  1. Launch Apprunner
  2. Click here to open a small .gif image.
  3. Click here to open a large .gif image.
  4. Click here to open a small .jpg image.
  5. Click here to open a largel .jpg image.
  6. Click here to open a .xbm image.
  7. Click here to open an animated .gif image.
  8. Select the menu option "FILE | PRINT" to print the image
  9. Press "OK" to print the image.
  10. Similarly, print all the images one by one.

Expected Results

All images should print properly.
