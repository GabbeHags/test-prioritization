Test Case Description for open/close of sidebar

Purpose

Determine that Sidebar opens and closes properly.

Initial Conditions

None.

Steps/Description

  1. Launch the browser
  2. Point and click with the mouse on te grippy at the center of the Sidebar border
  3. Clicking once should close the sidebar
  4. Again, point with the mouse on the grippy on the left border of the browser window and click the mouse
  5. This should open the Sidebar with the original width

Expected Results

Sidebar should open/close properly with grippy
