Test Case Description for deleting all panels

Purpose

Determine that all panels can be deleted from the Sidebar

Initial Conditions

None.

Steps/Description

  1. Launch the browser
  2. From the Sidebar , select "Tabs | Customize Sidebar"
  3. From the window that opens up, from the pane titled "My Sidebar", click with the mouse on the bottommost panel
  4. Click on "Remove" button once for each panel to remove all the panels
  5. Press "OK" to close the window

Expected Results

After step 4, the pane on the right should be blank. After step 5, no panels should be listed in the Sidebar and a mesage saying "The Sidebar is currently empty.....and select Sidebar" should appear.
