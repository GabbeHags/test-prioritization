Test Case Description for add-delete-ptoolbar-bookmark

Purpose

This basic testcase is designed to verify that bookmarks are added and deleted from the personal toolbar properly.

Initial Conditions

  • You must have a bookmarks file.
  • To promote consistency, you may choose to use the default set of Netscape Seamonkey Bookmarks.
  • You also have the option to use this very large bookmarks file.
  • You must also customize your sidebar to include a bookmarks panel.

Steps/Description

  1. Open the Manage Bookmarks Window.
  2. Edit|Cut or Edit|Copy any bookmark.
  3. Select the 'Personal Toolbar Folder'. Double-click it or single-click the triangle to open the folder.
  4. Select Edit|Paste.
  5. Go to a browser window and click once on the new link in the personal toolbar.

Expected Results

  1. The bookmark you added should appear immediately on the personal toolbar in a browser window and be activated by a single click.
  2. It should also be present as a subitem under "Personal Toolbar Folder" in the toplevel Bookmarks menu, in the bookmarks popup within the personal toolbar itself, and in the bookmarks sidepanel.
