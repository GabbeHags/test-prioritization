Test Case Description for panel title

Purpose

Determine that panel title appears in the Tabs menu when a panel is added

Initial Conditions

None.

Steps/Description

  1. Launch the browser
  2. From the Sidebar, select "Tabs | Customize Sidebar"
  3. Highlight any panel from the pane on the left and press the "Add" button to add the panel
  4. Press "OK" to close the window
  5. Now, click on the Tabs button in the Sidebar to open up a menu that lists all panels in the Sidebar
  6. The panel that you just added should also be listed (title shown) in the menu listing

Expected Results

Added panel should be listed in the Tabs menu
