Test Case Description for X Bitmap (XBM) format images

Purpose

Confirm that X Bitmap (XBM) format images can be displayed.

XBM will not be supported in the initial 5.0 release. This test case may need to be obsoleted.

Initial Conditions

None.

Steps/Description

  1. Launch Netscape 6
  2. View the test page (linked below)
  3. Independently view each of the two images files (also linked below)

Expected Results

After step 1, the web page displayed should exhibit display a black & white fancy bitmap image of a clock, followed below by a black and white bitmap image of a penguin.

After step 2, both the clock and penguin images should be displayed, identically to their appearance in step 1 (or as viewed by Communicator 4.x).
