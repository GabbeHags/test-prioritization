Test Case Description for customizing panels

Purpose

Determine that panels can be customized

Initial Conditions

None.

Steps/Description

  1. Launch the browser
  2. From the Sidebar, select "Tabs | Customize Sidebar"
  3. From the window that opens up, select and click on a panel, say, Stocks and press the Add button to add the panel to the Sidebar
  4. Highlight the Stocks panel from the right pane titled "My Sidebar" and click on the "Customize Panel..." button
  5. A Netscape Registration window should open up
  6. If you already have a netcenter username, press the "Sign in" button else fill the form details and press "Next"
  7. Proceed with the registration. A "Welcome" window giving you options to customize your page will open up
  8. Clicking on "Build My Page!" button should open a browser window showing all panels for your customized webpage
  9. From the "Stocks" window, clicking on the "smiling face" icon should open a customize window for Stocks
  10. Add a few stocks to your portfolio and make any changes
  11. Click on the "Save" button to clsoe the window

Expected Results

All your customized changes should be visible in the panel in the Sidebar
