Test Case Description for gif87a format images

Purpose

To confirm that that GIF 87a images are properly displayed across different bit depths.

Initial Conditions

None.

Steps/Description

  1. Launch Netscape 6
  2. View the test page (linked below)

Expected Results

  1. A mozilla.org banner, displayed at a descending bit depth, starting at 8-bit, and ceasing at 3-bit, followed by a grayscale image.
  2. A Hubble space image, displayed at a descending bit depth, starting at 8-bit, and ceasing at 3-bit, followed by a grayscale image.
  3. 5 rows of a single black pixel.
