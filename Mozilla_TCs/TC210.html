Test Case Description for order and panel list saving

Purpose

Determine that order and panel list gets saved

Initial Conditions

None.

Steps/Description

  1. Launch the browser
  2. From the Sidebar, select "Tabs | Customize Sidebar"
  3. From the window that opens up, add a few panels from the left panel (or delete a few panels)
  4. Highlight any of the panels in the right pane and click on the reorder arrows on the right side to move the panel up/down in the panels list
  5. Press the "OK" button to close the window and apply your changes to the Sidebar

Expected Results

Verify that the panel changes and reordering gets reflected in the Sidebar when you press the OK button
