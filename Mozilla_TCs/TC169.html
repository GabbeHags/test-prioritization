Test Case Description for printing large pages

Purpose

Determine that Seamonkey prints large complex pages properly

Initial Conditions

None

Steps/Description

  1. Launch Apprunner
  2. Click here to open a large page with HTML text
  3. Select menu option "FILE | PRINT"
  4. Press "OK" to print the page
  5. Click here to open a large, complex web page
  6. Repeat steps 2 & 3.

Expected Results

After Step 3 & 5, both pages should print properly.
