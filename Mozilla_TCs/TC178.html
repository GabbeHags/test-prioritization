Test Case Description for portrait and landscape settings

Purpose

Determine that Seamonkey prints pages with portrait and landscape settings properly

Initial Conditions

None

Steps/Description

  1. Launch Apprunner
  2. Click here to view a sample page
  3. Select menu option "FILE| PRINT"
  4. Click on the "Properties" button.
  5. Under the "Page Setup" option, in the frame titled "Orientation", select the radio button titled "Portrait"
  6. Press OK twice to print the page
  7. Repeat step 1 thru 3 and select the radio button titled "Landscape" under the "Orientation" Frame.
  8. Press "OK" twice to print the page

Expected Results

After Step 6, the page should be printed in Portrait setting

After Step 8, the page should be printed in Landscape setting
